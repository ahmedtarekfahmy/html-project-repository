    • item Expectation
    The global economic By 2030, AI is expected to contribute the total global economy with about 14% which equivalent to 15.7 trillion US$.
    The global revenue The year 2020 is expected to witness 1.2 trillion US$ as global revenue of insights-driven businesses.
    Jobs The World Economic Forum predicts that by 2022, around 122 million new jobs will be created. At the same time, AI statistics predict 75 million job positions will be removed. People will need to update their knowledge and skills.
    Costumer services At the moment, 85% of customer service is done by AI. The predictions are that by the beginning of 2030, customer service will be completely AI controlled globally.
    Some related goods 20 million robots are expected in operating worldwide by 2030, 168.6 million of smart home devices shipped worldwide in the first quarter of 2019, up 37.3% from the previous year and 727,000 of agricultural robots forecast to ship in 2025, up from 60,000 units in 2018.
