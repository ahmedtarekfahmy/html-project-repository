    • Benefits:

      * In Economy, Business, and Industries:

        Artificial intelligence can push the work evolution upward. As the AI machines more smart as the business' efficiency increase and the probability of human-error decrease. The academic research, health sciences, and tech companies will enjoy enhanced capabilities.

      * In Humanity and Society:

        AI will help people to create new opportunities and enhance their lifestyle. AI-powered smart homes and provide better security. AI will help people solve more significant social problems and quality of life will get better. Health care services will be better at diagnostics and treatments.

      * In Justice System:

        Push the work evolution upward. As the AI machines smarter as the business' efficiency increase and the probability of human-error decrease.

    Risks:

    • AI is programmed to do something devastating: Autonomous weapons in the wrong hand could easily cause mass casualties or may be lead to an AI war that also results in mass casualties. This fear is more with the future more powerful AI.
    • AI does something good but may be develops a destructive method for achieving its goal: surely this can occur if we cannot completely adjust the AI’s goals as planned exactly.
